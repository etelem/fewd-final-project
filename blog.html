  • So – you hate your job. Why? Is it because you don’t like your actual work tasks? Perhaps. But if you dig deeper, there may be more to it than that. Does your boss treat you well and recognize you when you go that extra mile for an assignment? Do you have autonomy – are you allowed to work from home occasionally, or take time off? Do you have the tools you need – and a great physical working space? If not you may be part of a company that is not working to get the best out of you. The good news? You can actually help change the culture of your company. Now if your boss is a jerk – there may only be so much you can do. But truly – you putting a little bit of effort can work wonders for yourself, your co-workers, and ultimately the performance of your company. Try using our culture generator to get ideas. As per the energy project – people become engaged via4 categories – mental/emotional/physical/spiritual. They are yearning to feel like they a part of something, and you can help cultivate that feeling. Be nice. Plan small gathering and events. Celebrate birthdays, holidays, achievements. You will be shocked by how much a ‘vibe’ will be reciprocated.
